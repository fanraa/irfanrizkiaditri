About Me

Hello! I'm Irfan Rizki Aditri, but you can call me Fanra. I'm currently a Physics student at Institut Teknologi Sumatera (ITERA).

I have a deep passion for both physics and web development. I enjoy creating interactive simulations to visualize complex physics concepts and building games to apply my programming knowledge.

When I'm not studying or coding, you can find me exploring new technologies, reading about scientific discoveries, or experimenting with creative projects.

My goal is to bridge the gap between physics and technology by creating educational tools that make learning physics more engaging and accessible.
